About Construtora Nortesul

We have more than 37 years of solidity and reliability. Combining family tradition with professional business management focused on concrete results, we have become one of the most recognized construction companies in the city of Juiz de Fora, a reference in design, quality, punctuality, standard of finishes and profitability.

Our projects are carefully thought out at each stage of the work; from the foundation, through the structure to the finishing phase. Always valuing the essential items:

Quality:

In our constructions we choose the best materials, combining solidity, durability and aesthetics.

Construction timeline:

We seek not only to meet established deadlines, but to exceed customer expectations when delivering works.

Location:

We choose areas with a wide range of commerce and services and in great development for our projects, where the return for our customers is guaranteed.
